# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Contributors are expected to:

- communicate directly and respectfully
- focus criticism on code and behavior, not people
- avoid sharing private customer data or credentials
- keep discussions professional and technical

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment or personal attacks
- publishing private panorama data, secrets, or internal customer material
- abusive or discriminatory language
- deliberate disruption of collaboration or review

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit, remove, or reject content that violates these rules.

Serious incidents may be reported to:

- `developer@realsee.com`
